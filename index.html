Tajmahal

Jaipur

Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur is famous for its stunning palaces, forts, and vibrant bazaars, including the Hawa Mahal and Amer Fort.

Known for its stunning landscapes, high-altitude mountain passes, monasteries, and adventure activities like trekking and biking.
